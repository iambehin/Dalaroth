Version .05

  • General
    • Many new changes.
    • Dalaroth is on the way to being completey d20 driven.
    • We're going for a dnd feel now.
  • Combat
    • Mobs also are now on d20.
    • Str effects +tohit and +damage.
    • Dex affects armor class now.
    • Con increases hp.
    • Armor and Weapon stats can be viewed in a popup.
    • The item stats popup lets you look up other items, if you know their number.
    • You can also type in names, but for the moment, they are space sensitive: "Great Sword" will not return "Greatsword"
    • Fleeing encounters can now only be done by resetting your coordinates.
    • If you flee, the mob will be waiting for you the next time you leave a city.
  • Interface
    • There is now a tooltip bar at the bottom, which will give you advice on elements of the page, when you hover on them.
    • Minor Border changes.
    • Look for customizable borders sometime.
  • Upcoming:
    • Watch for a mage system soon.
    • Another city?

    • Initiatives (This is how ranged will be done, bows will get an extra shot or two before combat begins, if you pass a spot check.

  • Exploring is done differently now, you only get to walk on roads, or paths which I've coded, no more unlimited walking around.

    • Get credits when you level up.
    • Look for quests which give credits too.
    • Each credit can be used to increment a stat.
  • The Vahth Battle shop works, you can purchase items for battle.
    • Backpacks are now in, you can equip things from your bp.
    • Several new items.
    • Made all items(in bp or equiped) into a link which will popup a window displaying stats.
    • Armor chops off damage in combat.(damage_taken = damage_dealt-armor_value)
    • Eventually, armor will have con/str reqs.
  • Teleporter in Vahth works, get there by going n->n->w
  • Few new monsters.
  • Dex now gives you a chance to evade a random amount(based on dex) of damage in combat.
  • Look for mage system soon: mp(mystic points) shows now.
  • Storyline is developing, help still appreciated.
